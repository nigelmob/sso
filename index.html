How does it work?

We've got great prizes

As well as competing against the other halls to save energy, you can win prizes like Ben & Jerry’s (big tubs including vegan flavours!) and TOTUM cards for yourself by getting involved in our mini competitions throughout the year. These run once a fortnight and can usually be found on our social media pages.

Find your university team

To find out what prizes are on offer where you study, find your university here and check out the information on the overview page. If you’re not on Facebook you can sign-up to the mailing list for your uni via the ‘Get Involved’ tab on your university’s webpage.
